Proposed New Menu and Homepage

XP Investimentos

Objective

XP is a rapidly growing company, and as a result, they had to add a lot of content to their homepage, which can make navigation difficult.

Because of this, I created a proposed menu and homepage for XP, aiming to make navigation easier for the user.

The proposed menu brings together relevant information into simple categories, allowing users to find what they need with ease. The homepage was designed to highlight XP's main features, facilitating the discovery of new content and services.

Additionally, the page was developed to make the user experience more intuitive, with interactive elements that help users find what they are looking for quickly.

Prototype

Mobile interface focused on usability and quick access to investments and banking services.
