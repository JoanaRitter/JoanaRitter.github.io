Case Study · Mobile App

Attar

Professional relocation in an agile and innovative way

Design Thinking UX Research Prototyping Mobile App Personas

Objective

Help with professional relocation and career improvement

Methodology

Design Thinking · 4 phases

Product

Mobile digital platform with jobs, courses and selection

Overview
EN-US

Searching for the ideal job and keeping up to date has never been more popular than it is today.

We created this digital platform with several opportunities, as dissemination of free courses, helping in professional and intellectual improvement, directing people to the respective jobs and selection processes.

Through this app, relocation to the job market has become more dynamic, faster and less stressful.

Objective: Helping people looking for professional improvement or a return to the job market in an agile and effective way.

Challenge: Understand people's needs during this delicate period and get them to join an app that can help in an innovative way.

Design Thinking was the methodology used. A method that uses design as a structured approach to generate and improve ideas. Methodology carried out in four phases, which goes from identifying the challenge to solving the problem.

Methodology
01
Empathy

Deeply understand the pains, needs and behaviors of users.

02
Definition

Synthesize what was learned and clearly define the problem to be solved.

03
Ideation

Generate and explore various creative solutions for the identified challenge.

04
Prototype

Materialize ideas into testable and functional prototypes for validation.

Research
EN-US

We interviewed people who helped us measure which information and functions would be paramount for our application.

Based on the surveys, we created two personas, one with an entrepreneurial profile, with numerous experiences and one that has no experiences, as it is starting his professional life. Both represent our ideal customer and facilitate the direction of product construction.
