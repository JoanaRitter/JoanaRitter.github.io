INFLUO

Transforming creator marketing with artificial intelligence

The Challenge

Finding the ideal creator is still a slow, manual process based on trial and error. Existing tools require complex filters but ignore an essential point: users don't know exactly what to look for.

The Question

How to transform a complex problem into a simple and intelligent experience?

  • • Slow
  • • Manual
  • • Trial and Error

Discovery & Research

We started with a common hypothesis: users would find creators using filters. However, during interviews with marketing professionals and small brands, we realized that users think in objectives, not in parameters.

Key Learnings

"I know the campaign objective, but I don't know where to start"
"Filters don't guarantee good results"
"Testing creators takes too much time"
💡

Insight

The problem is not searching for creators.
It's transforming intention into decision.

The Solution

We completely changed the approach: from a filter-based search to an AI-guided experience. We created an interface where users describe their campaign in natural language and the system interprets intention, analyzes context, and recommends creators based on compatibility.

Interpretation

Use of NLP to understand the campaign briefing beyond keywords.

Context

Analysis of niche, audience, and the creator's real engagement relative to the goal.

Compatibility

Match score based on historical data and semantics.

Design Decision

Transforming the search into a conversational experience allowed us to reduce cognitive load, eliminate friction, and align the system with the user's mental model.

Experience Structure

  • → Sidebar menu for navigation
  • → AI Chat as the main entry point
  • → Dynamic results on the side

Prototyping & Testing

The solution evolved in rapid cycles: from filter searches to real-time results chat. We conducted 4 qualitative tests with real users.

Test Results

Preference for chat vs. filters 100%

• Perceptible reduction in interface understanding time.
• Increased trust when AI explained the recommendations.

Iterations

Based on testing, we refined the product:

  • + Reduced information in cards
  • + Highlighted the compatibility score
  • + Improved spacing and legibility
  • + Simplified visual hierarchy

The Role of the Design Engineer

I acted as Design Engineer throughout the process: discovery, research, UX/UI, interaction design, prototyping, and AI-enabled front-end development. Integrating design and code allowed for faster iterations and more precise decisions.

Engineering + Design

AI was used both within the product and in building the interface, ensuring consistency and performance in the final experience.

A platform that allows discovering creators with natural language, receiving data-driven recommendations, and making clearer decisions.
